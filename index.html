About Us

Our Story

Ka Fa Bogaleng (Pty) Ltd is a 100% black owned female company founded on the 02 July 2019 in Tsantsabane. We are a multi-dimensional company rendering different services across various industries with a commitment to excellence and empowerment.

Our Mission

Render flawless services to our clients, build and maintain long lasting customer relationships and become partners of choice in all the industries we operate in.

Our Vision

To inject matriarchy to the boardroom, stir feminism in trade, birth hope, and create long lasting legacies that transform communities.

Our Core Values

  • Family life and community
  • Sustainable development
  • Ethical business conduct
  • Innovative solutions
  • Lasting partnerships
